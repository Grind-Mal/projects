# Contributing to GrindMal Projects

Thanks for contributing! All contributions go through the **GrindMal Review Guidelines**.

## Steps to Contribute

1. Pick a project from `projects.json` or issues.
2. Fork the repo & work on your branch.
3. Submit a PR describing your change and reasoning.
4. Reviews will follow the rules in [REVIEW.md].
5. Approved PRs are merged, rejected ones should be improved & resubmitted.

> Remember: reviews are artifacts, not opinions. Low-effort or opinion-only PRs may be ignored.
